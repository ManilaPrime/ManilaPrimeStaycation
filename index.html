House Rules

To ensure a comfortable and pleasant stay for everyone, please observe the following guidelines.

General House Rules

No Smoking

Smoking is strictly prohibited inside the unit. A cleaning fee of ₱2,000 will be charged for violations.

Quiet Hours

Please observe quiet hours from 10:00 PM to 8:00 AM. Respect other residents in the building.

Guest Limit

Only registered guests are allowed to stay overnight. Maximum occupancy is strictly enforced.

No Pets

We love animals, but pets are not allowed in the unit to maintain cleanliness and prevent allergies.

No Parties

Parties and events are not permitted. The unit is for residential use only.

Cleanliness

Please maintain cleanliness during your stay. Dispose of trash properly in designated areas.

Check-in & Check-out Policies

Check-in Process

Standard Check-in Time

Check-in time is from 2:00 PM onwards. Early check-in may be available upon request, subject to availability.

Self Check-in

Detailed check-in instructions will be provided before your arrival. Please follow them carefully.

ID Verification

All guests must provide valid ID for verification purposes. This is for security and building regulations.

Check-out Process

Standard Check-out Time

Check-out time is by 12:00 PM (noon). Late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability.

Check-out Procedure

Please ensure all windows are closed, lights and appliances are turned off, and the door is locked when leaving.

Key Return

Return keys as instructed in the check-out information. Lost keys will incur a replacement fee.

Safety & Security Guidelines

Lock Up

Always lock doors and windows when leaving the unit, even for short periods. Your security is important to us.

Visitor Policy

All visitors must be registered with building security. Unregistered guests will not be allowed entry.

Fire Safety

Familiarize yourself with emergency exits and fire safety equipment. Do not tamper with smoke detectors.

Electrical Safety

Turn off all appliances when not in use. Do not overload electrical outlets with multiple devices.

Emergency Contacts

Emergency contact information is provided in the unit. Please contact us immediately for any security concerns.

Access Cards/Keys

Do not share access cards or keys with non-registered guests. Report lost cards/keys immediately.

Amenities Usage Guidelines

Swimming Pool

Hours: 6:00 AM - 10:00 PM
Rules: Shower before entering. No diving. Children must be supervised at all times. No food or glass containers in the pool area.

Fitness Center

Hours: 24/7 access
Rules: Use equipment properly. Clean equipment after use. Wear appropriate athletic footwear. No children under 16 without adult supervision.

Garden & Leisure Areas

Hours: 6:00 AM - 10:00 PM
Rules: Keep noise levels reasonable. Clean up after yourself. No smoking. Respect the plants and facilities.

Elevators & Common Areas

Rules: Keep common areas clean. Do not block elevators or hallways. Be considerate of other residents. Follow all posted signs and instructions.

Important Note

Your access to building amenities is a privilege. Failure to follow the rules may result in restricted access. Please be respectful of other residents and maintain the facilities for everyone's enjoyment.

Compliance with Rules

By booking and staying at Manila Prime Staycation, you agree to comply with all house rules. Failure to follow these guidelines may result in additional charges, early termination of your stay without refund, or being banned from future bookings.

We appreciate your cooperation in maintaining a pleasant environment for all guests. If you have any questions about our policies, please don't hesitate to contact us.
